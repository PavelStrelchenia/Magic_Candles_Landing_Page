Wide range of products.

Container candles: Poured into glasses, jars or plaster. Ideal for soft waxes (soy, coconut).

Molded candles: Made using molds (silicone, plastic, metal). They require hard materials (beeswax, stearin, paraffin) that hold their shape well.

Candles made from beeswax: The easiest way for beginners is to roll sheets of natural beeswax with a wick inside.

Gel candles: Transparent candles with decorations (shells, beads) placed inside by pouring a special gel into glass vessels.
